RAKSHA AI — Digital Public Safety Intelligence

Digital arrest scams cost Indian families ₹1,031.9 crore in 2024 alone. RAKSHA AI is a free, AI-powered defense platform that detects and prevents digital arrest scams, counterfeiting, and organized fraud networks in India. Built with React, TypeScript, and multi-agent AI analysis for real-time transcript analysis, threat detection, and citizen protection.

Digital Arrest Scam Detection

The Digital Arrest Scam Detector provides real-time transcript analysis during phone calls. Choose from 39 pre-loaded scam scenarios covering digital arrest fraud, tech support scams, bank impersonation, tax authority fraud, and more. The detector runs a multi-agent analysis pipeline with specialized AI agents including a VoicePrint Agent for caller identity verification, an NLP Intent Agent for speech pattern analysis, an Entity Extraction Agent for identifying personal and financial data requests, a Financial Flow Agent for tracking money transfer demands, an Isolation Pattern Agent for detecting coercion tactics, and a Threat Escalation Agent for measuring urgency pressure.

Multi-Agent Analysis Pipeline

The Analysis tab displays real-time transcript analysis with detection precision, recall rate, false positive rate, and alert lead time metrics. Each detected red flag is timestamped and attributed to the specific agent that identified it. The system achieves 97.3% detection accuracy by cross-referencing multiple analysis signals from six specialized AI agents running in parallel. Risk levels range from Critical (Dangerous) through High (Suspicious), Medium (Caution), to Low (Safe), each with tailored response guidance and specific recommended actions for the user to follow.

AI-Powered Insights and Deep Analysis

The AI Insights tab provides threat classification with confidence scoring, scam probability assessment, and risk level determination. The AI Deep Analysis section explains the psychological tricks used by scammers, including authority impersonation, urgency creation, isolation tactics, and financial pressure. It lists safe actions the user should take, explains what victims usually do wrong, and provides specific prevention advice for each scam type.

Safety Recommendations and Next Steps

The Safety tab provides risk-level-specific safety tips. Critical risk scenarios trigger immediate action items including calling the 1930 Cybercrime Helpline, alerting your bank, and filing a report on cybercrime.gov.in within 24 hours. High risk scenarios advise verifying caller identity through official channels. Medium and low risk scenarios provide preventive guidance on two-factor authentication, transaction alerts, and monitoring. The What To Do Next section provides prioritized, urgent-flagged action items tailored to each risk level.

Learning Mode and Evidence Export

The Learn tab breaks down scam transcripts step by step, explaining the hook, building pressure, the ask, red flags identified, and how to stay safe. The Evidence Package module generates exportable evidence files with RAKSHA AI analysis headers, timestamps, and IT Act Section 65B compliance notes for legal admissibility. The Knowledge tab provides a searchable library of scam patterns, prevention guides, and regulatory information.

Fraud Message and Call Checking

The Citizen Fraud Shield lets you check any suspicious WhatsApp message, SMS, or IVR call in seconds. Paste a message or describe a call, and the AI instantly evaluates phishing indicators, urgency language, suspicious links, and social engineering tactics. Available in Hindi, English, and regional languages for accessibility across India.

Counterfeit Currency Verification

The Counterfeit Currency Checker uses RBI-standard security feature verification for ₹500 and ₹2000 banknotes. Upload a photo of any banknote to receive confidence scoring based on security thread analysis, watermark verification, color-shifting ink detection, and micro-printing inspection. Protect yourself from counterfeit currency circulating in local markets.

Citizen Safety and Protection

The Safety Center provides emergency contacts, a personalized safety score, and quick-access protection tools. The Live Scam Alerts module delivers real-time trending scam alerts and threat warnings across India, helping citizens stay informed about new fraud schemes targeting their region.

Fraud Reporting and Complaints

The NCRP Report Filing module enables citizens to file complaints directly to NCRB and cybercrime.gov.in with auto-generated evidence packages. Call the 1930 Cybercrime Helpline for immediate assistance with financial fraud. Report incidents to the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal for official investigation.

Threat Intelligence and Crime Mapping

The Threat Intelligence module provides AI-powered mapping of organized fraud operations and scam networks. The Fraud Network Graph uses force-directed graph analysis to expose coordinated fraud rings linking scammer numbers, UPI IDs, and wallets. The Crime Heatmap offers geospatial crime density visualization for patrol prioritization across Indian cities.

About RAKSHA AI

Digital arrest scams are a growing crisis in India. Scammers impersonate law enforcement officials and coerce victims into transferring money under the threat of arrest. In 2024 alone, Indian families lost over ₹1,031.9 crore to these fraud schemes. RAKSHA AI combines multi-agent AI analysis, real-time threat detection, and citizen protection tools to combat this wave of cybercrime. The platform uses specialized AI agents for voice analysis, natural language processing, entity extraction, financial flow tracking, and threat escalation detection. Built with React and TypeScript, RAKSHA AI is free to use and open-source, serving as a digital public safety tool for every Indian citizen. If you or someone you know has been targeted, contact the 1930 Cybercrime Helpline immediately or file a report at cybercrime.gov.in.
